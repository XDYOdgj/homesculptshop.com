Ridgid 59797 K-6 Toilet Auger with Bulb Head

The Ridgid 59797 K-6 Toilet Auger is a reliable and efficient solution for clearing stubborn toilet clogs, including those in modern water-conserving fixtures and Water Sense toilets. Designed for both home and professional use, this auger ensures damage-free unclogging thanks to its innovative vinyl guard, which protects the porcelain at the throat of the s-bend. The durable construction and ergonomic handles make it comfortable and easy to use, even during long or frequent plumbing tasks.

With a 6-foot length and a 1/2-inch (12.7 mm) reinforced cable, this toilet clog remover is built to tackle tough blockages, such as tangled rags, tree roots, or loose debris. The three-layer compression-wrapped cable adds strength and kink resistance, allowing the auger to reach deep into pipes and navigate tight spaces without losing power. The snap-on design makes it simple to switch out the cable as needed, offering flexibility and convenience for daily use.

A great choice for anyone looking to maintain clear and trouble-free plumbing, this Ridgid toilet auger delivers consistent performance while ensuring your bathroom stays in top condition. Whether you're dealing with small clogs or major blockages, the K-6 model provides the right tools for the job, helping you save time and effort with every use.

Key Features

  • Protects porcelain with a durable vinyl guard
  • 3-layer compression-wrapped cable for enhanced strength and kink resistance
  • 6-foot length with 1/2-inch (12.7 mm) auger snake for deep pipe cleaning
  • Ergonomic handle for user comfort and ease of operation
  • Corrosion-resistant tube and quick snap-on cable attachment for convenience

Why Choose Ridgid 59797 K-6?
