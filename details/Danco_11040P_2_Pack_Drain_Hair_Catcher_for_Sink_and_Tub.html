Danco 11040P Drain Hair Catcher

Keep your bathroom drains free from clogs with the Danco 11040P Drain Hair Catcher. This essential kitchen and bathroom accessory is engineered to stop hair, soap scuds, dirt, and debris from blocking your drains, helping you maintain a clean and functional space with ease.

The non-slip rubber outer ring ensures a secure fit, keeping hair contained and reducing the risk of buildup. Its brushed nickel finish adds a touch of sophistication to your fixtures, combining practicality with style. Made from sturdy stainless steel, this drain protector is built to last, providing reliable performance over years of use in high-traffic areas.

Why Choose the Danco 11040P?

  • Prevents clogs caused by hair, soap, dirt, and debris
  • Non-slip rubber design to secure hair in the drain opening
  • Fits most standard sink and tub drains up to 2.75 inches in diameter
  • Brushed nickel finish for a modern, stylish look
  • Durable stainless steel construction for long-lasting use

This 2-pack is ideal for households with long hair or those who want to avoid the hassle of regular drain maintenance. The easy-to-clean design allows for quick removal of trapped debris, saving you time and effort. A great addition to your bathroom setup, it ensures your drains stay clear without compromising on aesthetics.

Danco 11040P Drain Hair Catcher is a versatile and durable solution for both bathroom sink and bathtub drains. Its combination of functionality, style, and reliability makes it a top choice for homeowners seeking an easy way to prevent costly clogs.
