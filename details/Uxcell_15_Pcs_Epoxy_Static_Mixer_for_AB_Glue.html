Precision Mixing Made Easy with Uxcell Static Mixers

Whether you're a professional or a DIY enthusiast, these Uxcell static mixers are the perfect solution for achieving consistent, accurate mixing results with two-component adhesives. Designed with a spiral internal mechanism, they ensure thorough blending of materials, even when viscosity levels differ, making them essential for any bonding task.

Why Choose Uxcell Static Mixers?

Engineered for durability and reliability, these mixers feature a high-quality PE resin construction with a transparent shell, allowing you to clearly observe the mixing process and ensure precision every time. The square interface ensures a secure fit with 50mL cartridges, eliminating leaks and promoting smooth, even application of your mixed adhesive.

With a 1:1 or 2:1 mixing ratio option, they offer flexibility to meet various application requirements. Each set includes 15 mixing tubes, perfect for single-use and easy cleanup. Their reusability and cost-effective design make them a smart investment for both beginners and experienced users alike.

Versatile and Reliable for All Applications

These mixers are ideal for use with a wide range of substrates, including metals, thermoset composites, and more. Whether you're working on industrial projects, automotive repairs, or home improvement tasks, Uxcell static mixers deliver unmatched efficiency and performance.

Experience the difference with a high-quality, user-friendly mixing solution that brings precision to every project.
