Kigai Microfiber Slippers with Paisley Pattern

These Kigai Microfiber Slippers are the ultimate blend of comfort and practicality. Designed to make your daily life easier, they combine softness with functionality, making them a great choice for any home. The plush material, made from high-quality cotton and chenille, feels gentle against your skin and is perfect for long hours of wear. Whether you're lounging on the couch or moving around the house, they provide the support and cushion you need.

With their universal M and L sizes, these slippers are ideal for a wide range of foot types, from sizes 5-8 to 9-12.
