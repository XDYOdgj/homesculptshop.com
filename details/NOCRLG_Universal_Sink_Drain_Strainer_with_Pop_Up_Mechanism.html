NOCRLG Universal Sink Drain Strainer

Designed for everyday use in your bathroom, this reliable sink drain strainer keeps your water flowing smoothly while preventing common clogs. The built-in hair catcher basket is made from durable stainless steel to trap hair, soap scum, and other debris, keeping your sink clean and functional.

With a user-friendly pop-up mechanism, this drain stopper allows for effortless opening and closing, making it easy to control water flow. The removable design simplifies cleaning, ensuring you can maintain your sink with minimal effort. Built to last, this strainer is crafted from high-quality ABS and thickened copper, providing long-term use and rust prevention for your bathroom fixtures.

Key Features

  • Efficient clogging prevention with built-in hair catcher basket
  • Easy pop-up operation for quick drainage control
  • Removable stainless steel filter for hassle-free cleaning
  • Universal fit for most U.S. bathroom sinks and drains
  • Durable construction with high-quality ABS and thickened copper materials

Why Choose This Product?

This universal replacement drain is perfect for both bathroom sink drains and shower tub drains. It fits most U.S. sink sizes with a drain inner diameter ranging from 1.1" to 1.3". The combination of efficient clogging prevention and easy maintenance makes it a great solution for any home looking for reliable, long-lasting drainage accessories.

Whether you're worried about slow drains or messy buildup, this NOCRLG drain strainer offers the perfect blend of performance, durability, and ease of use for your daily bathroom routine.
