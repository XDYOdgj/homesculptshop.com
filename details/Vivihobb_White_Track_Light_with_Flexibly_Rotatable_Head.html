Modern & Elegant Adjustable Track Lighting with Rotatable Head

Enhance your space with the Vivihobb White Track Light, a stylish and functional lighting solution designed for versatility. Its classic white finish and elegant lampshade blend seamlessly into any interior decor, whether it's a cozy bedroom or a contemporary living room. With a rotatable light head, you can adjust the direction and intensity of the light to meet your specific needs, making it perfect for reading, cooking, or showcasing art.

Key Features

  • Classic white finish with elegant lampshade design
  • 3-spot lighting system for focused illumination
  • 270-degree horizontal and 180-degree vertical rotation
  • E26 base compatible with standard US junction boxes

Why Choose This Track Light?

This modern track lighting fixture is thoughtfully crafted to bring both style and practicality to your home or office.
