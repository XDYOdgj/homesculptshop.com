Simba Lighting 10W MR11 Halogen Spotlight Bulbs

Elevate your lighting with these high-performance MR11 halogen spotlight bulbs that deliver bright and warm illumination. Designed for a rich, inviting ambiance, they feature a 2700K color temperature and 110 lumens of light output, ensuring a soft yet powerful glow for any room.

Exceptional Features for Every Lighting Need

  • 10 Watt High Output with 110 Lumens for Bright, Energy-Efficient Lighting
  • 12V Low Voltage AC or DC Design for Versatile Use in Home and Commercial Settings
  • 30° Beam Angle for Targeted Accent Lighting and Decorative Effects
  • Dimmable Warm White 2700K Light with CRI 100 for Natural and Soft Illumination
  • Aluminum Housing with Glass Cover for Durable, Long-Lasting Performance

Perfect for a Variety of Lighting Applications

These 2-pack spotlight bulbs are ideal for track lights, accent lighting setups, fiber optics, and even holiday light displays. Their narrow beam ensures focused light that highlights artwork, architectural details, and interior decor with precision and style.

Built to last, these bulbs have a long lifespan of up to 2,000 hours, offering consistent performance for residential and commercial environments. With a 1.38" diameter and MR11 reflector design, they fit seamlessly into existing fixtures while maintaining an easy installation process through their GU4 bi-pin base.

Safe, Reliable, and Eco-Friendly

CE and RoHS compliant, these bulbs are not only safe for use but also contribute to an environmentally conscious lighting choice. They are mercury-free and designed for use indoors and outdoors, making them a versatile solution for all your lighting needs.

Enjoy peace of mind with a 90-day warranty on these premium halogen bulbs, ensuring quality and satisfaction with every purchase. Whether you're lighting your home, office, or an event space, these bulbs are the perfect blend of style, performance, and affordability.
