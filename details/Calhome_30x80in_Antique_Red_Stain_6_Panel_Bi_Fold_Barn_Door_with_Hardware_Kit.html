Elegant Bi-Fold Barn Door with Vintage Red Stain

Enhance your home's style and functionality with this exquisite Calhome 30 in. x 80 in. bi-fold barn door. Crafted from high-quality MDF, it offers the durability of solid wood without the cost or maintenance, making it ideal for both indoor and outdoor spaces.

Durable and Long-Lasting

Thanks to its fade-resistant multi-step paint and seal finish, this door maintains vibrant colors even when exposed to sunlight or changing weather conditions.
