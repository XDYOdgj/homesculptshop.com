BalanceFrom Smart Scale

A precise, stylish, and versatile digital weight scale that helps you stay on track with your health and fitness goals.

Precision Meets Convenience

The BalanceFrom Smart Scale is built with 4 high-precision sensors that deliver accurate weight measurements down to 0.2 pounds and 0.1 kilograms. This level of detail ensures you can monitor your progress with confidence, no matter where you're on your fitness journey.

With a 400lb capacity and 0.2lb increments, it's perfect for anyone looking for a reliable tool to track their health data accurately. The spacious, 12x12 inch platform is designed for comfort and stability, making it easier to step on and use consistently.

Track Your Progress with Ease

More than just a weight scale, this device also analyzes 12 body composition metrics, giving you a comprehensive view of your health. Whether you're weighing yourself at home or on the go, the BalanceFrom Smart Scale is designed to integrate seamlessly into your routine.

Its Bluetooth connectivity allows for easy synchronization with your preferred fitness app, keeping your health data organized and accessible. The easy-to-read LED display and the option to switch between pounds and kilograms make it a versatile choice for anyone monitoring their weight and body metrics.

Built for Longevity and Usability

The non-slip feet ensure the scale stays stable during each use, preventing movement or damage to your floors. It also features a tempered glass surface that's both durable and easy to clean, making it ideal for daily use in your bathroom, bedroom, or gym.

With automatic calibration and power-saving functions, this smart scale is designed for efficiency. It turns off automatically after a few seconds of inactivity, helping to extend battery life while keeping your health data accurate and up-to-date.
